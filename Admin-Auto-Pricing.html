Print Infrastructure Unlocks 42 hrs/yr
Shipping labels and packing slips require local printer access. Cloud-hosted automation cannot reach on-premises devices directly.
Action: Deploy print gateway service (PrintNode ~$20/mo) or local automation agent
Production Schedule Format Unlocks 53 hrs/yr
